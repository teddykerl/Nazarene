THE KITCHEN

Where the coast meets the grill.

On 14th Street in Sinkor, Fuzion D'Afrique fuses West African cooking with the char, the sauces, and the shared-plate energy of a modern grill house. Whole fish comes off the fire dressed in lime and pepper, cassava leaf and palm butter simmer low, and the fry station keeps the wings, plantain, and cassava coming out hot. It's a kitchen built for a full table and a longer night.

7days a week service

THE SOUP BOWL
